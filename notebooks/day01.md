Day 1: Understand Churn & Project Goal

Customer churn is about understanding how many existing customers are using your product or service, and who may stop using it from next month. Churn prediction is commonly used to identify customers likely to cancel a subscription or stop engaging with a product.  [amplitude](https://amplitude.com/explore/analytics/churn-prediction)

It helps a streaming platform understand whether customers are renewing their subscription or not. In subscription businesses, churn is often measured as non-renewal, cancellation, or inactivity over a defined time period.  [amplitude](https://amplitude.com/explore/analytics/churn-prediction)

Potential churn can be reduced by providing offers, discounts, or other retention actions. Customer behavior, usage patterns, subscription details, and payment history are commonly used to understand churn risk.  [stripe](https://stripe.com/resources/more/how-to-build-a-customer-churn-model-a-guide-for-businesses)

## Project

Build a **customer churn prediction ML model** that can predict which customers are likely to churn. Typical features include session time, viewing behavior, subscription details, and payment method.
