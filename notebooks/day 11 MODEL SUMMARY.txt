Day 11 – Model Summary

Algorithm chosen: Logistic Regression with class balancing, tuned for churn detection.

Final hyperparameters: `C = 0.01`, `solver = liblinear`.

Fine-tuned metrics:
- Accuracy: 0.39378238341968913
- Precision: 0.21768707482993196
- Recall: 0.9411764705882353
- F1 Score: 0.35359116022099446
- Confusion Matrix: `[[44, 115], [2, 32]]`

Feature removal decision:
The following three features were removed from the final model:
- UserRating
- SupportTicketsPerMonth
- TotalCharges

Reason for removal:
These features showed weak or mixed contributions during feature testing, and removing them simplified the model without compromising the main goal of identifying churners.

Top important features and intuition:
- AccountAge: Short-tenure customers are more likely to churn.
- MonthlyCharges: Higher monthly cost can increase churn risk.
- ViewingHoursPerWeek: Lower usage often signals weaker engagement.
- ContentDownloadsPerMonth: Low download activity may indicate low interest.
- SubscriptionType: Basic plans may be more churn-prone than premium plans.
- ContentType: Content preference can reflect user engagement.
- DeviceRegistered: Device usage may affect customer behavior.
- GenrePreference: Strong preferences can indicate more stable engagement.

Final decision:
This is the model I will deploy.
